Features Documentation

Progress Hub

A dynamic, motivating tool designed to fuel your fitness journey and serve as a personal cheerleader.

Achievements (Trophy Case)

Engage and stay motivated with physical badges earned for completing challenges. Earn badges based on attendance, performance, and streaks.

  • Post-Workout Flow: Animated celebration UI upon earning a badge.
  • Badge States: Empty (not attempted), Inactive (in progress), Active (awarded).
  • Details View: Tap a badge to see completion criteria, dates, and related workouts.

Personal Records (PRs)

Track your top exercises and monitor strength growth over time. PRs are recorded for sets of 8 reps or more.

  • Recent PRs: Displays your 5 most recent achievements.
  • PR Detail Page: Interactive progress charts tracking improvements.
  • Push Further: Smart suggestions for recent workouts utilizing that specific exercise.

Classes & Home Tab

Your personalized hub for exploring new content, joining live sessions, and jumping into your next activity.

Browse & Search

Find exactly what you need with robust filtering. Search by activity (Strength, Core, Recovery, Yoga, HIIT, etc.) or duration (e.g., 10-minute quick sessions).

Strength Core Recovery HIIT Yoga

Live Classes

Integrated natively into the Classes tab via a dedicated carousel. Features include:

  • Join late and jump right into the live action.
  • Set reminders (10 min notifications).
  • Live countdowns starting 30 mins prior.

Home Tab Layout

Features a dynamic "Top Black Section" that highlights your very next scheduled activity based on active subscriptions, training plans, or daily readiness state.

Training Programs

Adaptable, structured plans that evolve with your progress and daily readiness.

Adaptable Training Plans

Programs tailored to your body’s needs, focusing on measurable strength gains rather than just completing workouts.

  • Body Readiness: Workouts adjust based on sleep quality, HRV, and muscle recovery.
  • Auto-Pause: If you are inactive for a week, your plan auto-pauses so you never fall behind.
  • AMRAP Tracking: Uses "As Many Reps As Possible" tests for weight loss and endurance plans.

Program Management

Total control over how you engage with structured content.

  • Join Multiple: Enroll in multiple programs simultaneously without pausing your current active program.
  • Visual Progress: Dynamic progress bars (e.g., 3/20 workouts completed) on Program Cards.
  • Upcoming Previews: View and set notifications for programs starting within 30 days.

Smart Features

AI-powered tracking, guidance, and feedback integrated directly into your workout experience.

Advanced Rep Counting

Uses depth sensors and AI to accurately track compound movements (squats, lunges, etc.), eliminating the need for manual counting.

Form Feedback

Compares your movements against ideal form to provide visual cues, correct mistakes, and prevent injury.

Range of Motion (ROM)

Pace meter technology that tracks time under tension and ensures you hit full depth on exercises like squats and deadlifts.

Weight Auto-ID

Automatically recognizes standard and extended weight plates based on color as soon as you complete 1 rep.

Dynamic Rest

Intelligently pauses your workout during rest periods if your heart rate remains too high, ensuring optimal recovery.

Reps in Reserve (RIR)

Prompts you to evaluate a set's difficulty by entering remaining potential reps, refining future weight recommendations.

Cloud Trainer Guidance

Real-time personalized coaching, weight adjustments, and encouragement delivered instantly based on live performance data.

Mirror Mode

A persistent front-facing camera toggle that stays on screen to help you check your positioning and form continuously.

Audio Cues

Helpful sound alerts 3 seconds before a set begins and 3 seconds before it ends, keeping your focus completely on form.

Phone Only Workout & BYOD

Take your fitness anywhere, using any equipment you have on hand.

Phone Only Workout

Access the full workout library directly from your device, no secondary screens required. Perfect for travel or quick sessions.

  • Camera Calibration: Simple red/green box interface ensures you are perfectly positioned before starting.
  • Streamlined UI: Leaderboards and previous rep counts are hidden to maximize screen real estate for the instructor and your form.
  • Flexible Tracking: Opt to work out without camera tracking if preferred (though it impacts data accuracy for adaptive plans).

Bring Your Own Dumbbells (BYOD)

You don't need proprietary weights to access world-class features. Customize your equipment loadout in the Pre-Class Settings.

Supported Equipment Types:
  • Standard (7.5 lb): Default intelligent weights.
  • Extended (10 lb): Heavier intelligent weights (rounded down in app for increment consistency).
  • 3rd Party Dumbbells: Use your own hex/adjustable weights. (Disables auto weight recognition).

Health & Community

Track profound body changes, understand your readiness, and workout with friends.

Body Composition Scan

A 3D reconstruction utilizing device depth sensors to accurately measure your physique.

  • Measures Body Fat %, Lean Mass, Body Circumference, and Waist-to-Hip ratio.
  • Delivers near-DXA scan accuracy right from your home.
  • Takes only 5-10 minutes to process into a full 3D color avatar.

Readiness Score

A 1-100 metric determining your physiological preparation for physical activity today.

  • Inputs: Sleep Quality, HRV, Resting Heart Rate, and localized Muscle Recovery.
  • States: Primed (80-100), Ready (60-80), Fatigued (40-60), Recovering (0-40).

Community Challenges

Create target or competition-based challenges for calories, volume lifted, or active minutes.

  • Shareable challenge links and IDs.
  • Dynamic leaderboards filterable by friends or overall community.

Referrals & Integrations

Grow your squad and sync your data everywhere.

  • Referrals: Generate a 30-day free trial code for friends. Earn a free month when they convert.
  • Strava & Apple HealthKit: Automatically sync completed workouts, HR data, and calories.

General Features & Settings

System-wide tools, accessibility settings, and customization.

Text Search

Available across Classes, PRs, and Training Plans. Features close-match logic to handle typos and requires a minimum of 3 characters for fast, relevant results.

Partial Credit

Workouts completed 50% or more now grant full completion credit. Partial metrics like calories and strength sets are dynamically awarded as you progress through the class.

Bluetooth Headphones

Connect your favorite Bluetooth headphones directly via the audio tray during a class or from pre-class settings for an immersive experience.

Closed Captioning (CC)

Improve accessibility and instruction comprehension. Toggled via workout preferences, CC sits bottom-center by default but dynamically repositions to avoid UI collision.

Pre-Class Settings & Volume Controls

Reduce setup friction. Access workout preferences before class starts to define your dumbbell type (Standard, Extended, 3rd Party) and adjust separate Coach vs. Music volume sliders.
